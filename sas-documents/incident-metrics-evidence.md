## Analysis: What The Data Proves

### 1. Response Time Dramatically Improved
- **Before Joana:** Only 48.84% of incidents met response SLA
- **Under Joana:** 78.72% of incidents met response SLA
- **Improvement:** Nearly 30 percentage point increase

### 2. Resolution Time Significantly Improved
- **Before Joana:** 79.07% of incidents met resolution SLA
- **Under Joana:** 95.74% of incidents met resolution SLA
- **Improvement:** Nearly 17 percentage point increase

### 3. SLA Breaches Reduced
- **Before Joana:** 6 resolution SLA breaches in the period
- **Under Joana:** Only 2 resolution SLA breaches
- **Improvement:** 67% reduction in breaches

### 4. Volume Increased (Expected with Product Growth)
- **Before Joana:** 43 created issues (Sep-Dec 2024)
- **Under Joana:** 47 created issues (Sep-Dec 2025)
- **Context:** More users, more features = expected increase in incidents
- **Key Point:** Despite increased volume, SLA performance improved significantly
